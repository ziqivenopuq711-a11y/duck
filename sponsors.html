Sponsors & Partners

The organizations, protocols, media outlets, and academic institutions powering Hack: AI Unchained

DuckChain — Founding Organizer

DuckChain is the founding organizer and primary host of the Hack: AI Unchained hackathon. As a Singapore-based blockchain platform at the frontier of AI-Web3 convergence, DuckChain brings deep technical expertise, a thriving developer ecosystem, and a genuine commitment to open innovation.

DuckChain contributes the lion's share of the prize pool — $1,000,000 in DUCK liquidity support and $100,000 in DUCK token rewards — along with infrastructure access, technical support, and mentorship for all participating teams. DuckChain's core engineering team is available throughout the hackathon for office hours, code reviews, and architecture consultations, ensuring that every team has the support they need to build something exceptional. Beyond the prize money, DuckChain's most significant contribution to AI Unchained is the platform itself: the infrastructure, tooling, and ecosystem that makes ambitious AI-blockchain projects buildable in a single week.

Amazon Web Services (AWS) — Co-Organizer

Amazon Web Services joins the AI Unchained hackathon as co-organizer and cloud infrastructure partner, contributing $10,000 in AWS cloud credits for finalist teams. AWS brings to the hackathon its full suite of AI/ML services, including Amazon SageMaker for model training and deployment, Amazon Bedrock for access to leading foundation models, AWS Lambda for serverless inference, and Amazon EC2 for scalable compute.

AWS solution architects will be available as mentors throughout the hackathon, helping teams design cloud-native architectures that complement their blockchain components. AWS's participation reflects the company's growing investment in the intersection of cloud computing and decentralized technology. For developers unfamiliar with AWS, DuckChain and AWS will co-host dedicated onboarding workshops at the start of the hackathon to help teams get their cloud environments configured and their first AI services running on AWS infrastructure.

Technology Partners

The AI Unchained hackathon benefits from a diverse ecosystem of technology partners who contribute tools, resources, and expertise. ElizaOS provides AI agent framework technology and development support. ChainGPT offers AI-powered smart contract auditing tools and development assistance. Mind Network brings privacy-preserving AI computation capabilities. Phala Network provides confidential computing infrastructure. Metis contributes Layer 2 scaling expertise.

Maxen supports the event with blockchain development tooling. Quack AI and Gata contribute AI agent infrastructure. InfinityGround provides distributed computing resources. LazAI contributes decentralized AI training infrastructure. Together, these partners ensure that hackathon participants have access to the cutting edge of both AI and blockchain technology. Each technology partner assigns dedicated team members to provide technical support to hackathon teams throughout the event.

Media & Community Partners

The reach and impact of AI Unchained is amplified by a strong network of media and community partners. In the blockchain media space, Coinness, TechFlow, BlockMedia, Foresight News, RootData, ChainCatcher, and BlockBeats provide editorial coverage and audience reach across millions of readers in the blockchain community.

Developer communities including OnePiece Labs, TinTinLand, BuidlerDAO, and MoleDAO spread the word within their networks of active builders. University blockchain clubs including Imperial Blockchain & FinTech, Singapore Uni DAO, LSE Blockchain, and blockchain societies at the University of Chicago and St Andrews provide academic reach and recruit talented student developers. This broad partnership network ensures that AI Unchained reaches every corner of the global developer community, from seasoned DeFi veterans to university students encountering blockchain for the first time.
