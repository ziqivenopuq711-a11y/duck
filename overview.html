Hackathon Overview

Everything you need to know about Hack: AI Unchained — the largest AI × Web3 hackathon of 2025

What is Hack: AI Unchained

Hack: AI Unchained is a global, week-long hackathon co-organized by DuckChain and Amazon Web Services. With a total prize pool exceeding $1.1 million, it stands as one of the largest AI-focused hackathons in the blockchain space. The event is designed to challenge developers to build innovative applications that sit at the intersection of artificial intelligence and decentralized technology.

Running entirely online from August 25 to September 1, 2025, the hackathon welcomes participants from every corner of the globe — from seasoned blockchain engineers to first-time AI developers. Teams of one to five members can register and compete across four distinct challenge tracks. Registration is free, entirely online, and requires no prior blockchain experience — DuckChain's comprehensive onboarding resources ensure that newcomers can get productive within hours of signing up.

Challenge Statement

The central challenge of Hack: AI Unchained is deceptively simple: build something that matters at the intersection of AI and blockchain. Participants are encouraged to think boldly about what becomes possible when AI agents can own wallets, sign transactions, and interact with smart contracts autonomously.

Can an AI agent manage a DeFi portfolio better than a human trader? Can decentralized AI infrastructure reduce the cost of model inference by an order of magnitude? Can on-chain AI-as-a-service democratize access to powerful language models for developers worldwide? These are the questions that AI Unchained dares participants to answer with working code. The best projects will not merely demonstrate technical cleverness — they will reveal genuine new possibilities that were previously impossible without the combination of AI and decentralized infrastructure.

Key insight: The most valuable projects will be those that are only possible at the AI × blockchain intersection — ideas that neither AI alone nor blockchain alone could achieve.

Key Technologies

Successful AI Unchained projects will typically leverage some combination of DuckChain's SDK, AWS cloud services, smart contract platforms, and AI agent frameworks. DuckChain provides a comprehensive developer toolkit including JavaScript/TypeScript SDK, Python SDK, smart contract templates, and testnet access with free gas.

AWS contributes SageMaker for model training, Bedrock for foundation model access, and Lambda for serverless AI inference. Participants can also integrate popular AI agent frameworks such as ElizaOS, LangChain, AutoGPT, and CrewAI with DuckChain's on-chain infrastructure. The combination of these tools gives developers everything they need to build sophisticated AI-blockchain applications during the hackathon window without worrying about infrastructure setup or access restrictions.

Who Should Participate

Hack: AI Unchained welcomes a diverse range of participants. Blockchain developers will find rich opportunities to build AI-enhanced smart contracts and protocols. AI/ML researchers can explore how their models perform in decentralized, trustless environments. Full-stack developers can build user-facing AI-powered dApps. Data scientists can work on on-chain data analysis and prediction markets.

Even designers and product managers are welcome — the best hackathon projects combine strong technical execution with clear product vision and great user experience. No prior blockchain experience is required; DuckChain's documentation and workshop sessions are designed to get newcomers up to speed quickly. Whether you are a solo developer or a coordinated team of five, whether you have been in crypto for years or are building on a blockchain for the first time, AI Unchained has a place for you.
