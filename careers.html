Careers at DuckChain

Join the team building the infrastructure for sovereign, AI-powered decentralized systems

Why Build with DuckChain

DuckChain is at the forefront of one of the most exciting technological frontiers of our time — the convergence of artificial intelligence and decentralized blockchain infrastructure. Based in Singapore, DuckChain is a remote-first, globally distributed team of builders who share a passion for open, sovereign, AI-powered systems.

At DuckChain, you will work on problems that matter: designing the infrastructure that the next generation of AI agents will run on, building tools that empower developers worldwide, and contributing to an ecosystem that challenges centralized monopolies over AI. The team is small enough that every person's contribution matters enormously, and large enough to tackle ambitious technical challenges. DuckChain's founders bring experience from leading blockchain protocols, AI research labs, and enterprise technology companies, creating a culture that blends academic rigor with startup speed and ambition.

Engineering Roles

DuckChain is actively recruiting across several engineering disciplines. Blockchain Engineers design and implement core protocol improvements, consensus mechanism enhancements, and AI-specific virtual machine features. Smart Contract Developers build audited contract libraries, DeFAI protocols, and developer-facing contract templates. AI/ML Engineers integrate machine learning models with on-chain systems, build model registries, and develop AI inference optimization techniques for blockchain environments.

Platform Engineers maintain developer infrastructure including the SDK, testnet, block explorer, and API services. Security Researchers perform smart contract audits, threat modeling, and penetration testing of the DuckChain protocol and ecosystem applications. All engineering roles at DuckChain come with significant autonomy, meaningful ownership over product direction, and the opportunity to contribute to one of the most technically novel systems in the blockchain industry today.

Product & Operations

Beyond engineering, DuckChain needs talented people in product and operations roles. Product Managers work closely with engineering to prioritize features, define roadmaps, and ensure that the platform meets the needs of developers and end users. Developer Relations (DevRel) Engineers bridge the gap between DuckChain's core team and the developer community through documentation, tutorials, conference presentations, and community support.

Community Managers build and nurture DuckChain's global developer community across Discord, Twitter, Telegram, and in-person events. Business Development professionals identify and develop strategic partnerships with AI platforms, blockchain protocols, and enterprise technology companies. These roles are critical to DuckChain's growth — the best technology in the world fails without the community, partnerships, and ecosystem relationships to support it. If you are passionate about the AI-blockchain frontier and love working directly with developers and builders, DuckChain has a role for you.

Culture & Benefits

DuckChain offers a culture built on autonomy, transparency, and shared ownership. As a crypto-native organization, team members receive meaningful token compensation in addition to competitive market salaries. Remote work is not just permitted — it is the default, with flexible hours that respect contributors in different time zones.

DuckChain invests heavily in professional development, providing generous education budgets, conference travel, and access to cutting-edge research. The team meets twice a year for in-person retreats that combine strategy sessions with genuine fun. DuckChain believes that the best teams are built through trust, and that the most innovative work happens when talented people have the freedom to pursue their best ideas. If you are excited by the prospect of contributing to the infrastructure that will underpin the next generation of AI-powered decentralized systems, we would love to hear from you.

Ready to apply? Send your background, relevant work samples, and a brief note on why DuckChain's mission resonates with you to careers@duckchain.io. We review all applications and respond within two weeks.
