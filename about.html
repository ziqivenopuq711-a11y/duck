About DuckChain

DuckChain is a Singapore-headquartered blockchain platform building next-generation infrastructure at the convergence of artificial intelligence and Web3. Founded with the vision of making blockchain truly useful in the age of AI, DuckChain delivers high-throughput, low-latency infrastructure designed specifically for AI workloads. Unlike traditional blockchains that were built for token transfers, DuckChain's architecture enables complex AI agent operations, real-time inference, and seamless integration with off-chain AI models.

The platform has processed millions of transactions while maintaining sub-second finality, making it the ideal foundation for developers who want to build AI-powered decentralized applications at scale. From DeFAI protocols to autonomous agent frameworks, DuckChain provides the primitives and tooling required to turn ambitious AI-blockchain concepts into production-ready applications that millions of users can rely on.

The AI Unchained Vision

DuckChain firmly believes that the future of AI belongs on decentralized rails. Centralized AI systems are vulnerable to censorship, single points of failure, and monopolistic control. By anchoring AI agents on DuckChain's blockchain infrastructure, developers can build autonomous systems that operate transparently, execute trustlessly, and remain resilient against interference.

The AI Unchained hackathon embodies this vision — creating space for builders to experiment with AI agents that manage funds, make governance decisions, and interact with DeFi protocols without human intermediaries. This is not just a hackathon; it is a movement toward sovereign, uncensorable intelligence. Every project built during AI Unchained contributes to a future where individuals and communities control their own AI systems, rather than delegating that power to opaque corporate platforms.

"This is where AI × Web3 innovation comes to life — the launchpad for the next generation of AI-native decentralized applications."

AWS Partnership

Amazon Web Services brings critical cloud infrastructure to the AI Unchained hackathon. AWS provides participating teams with cloud computing credits worth $10,000, access to AWS AI/ML services including Amazon SageMaker, Amazon Bedrock, and AWS Lambda, as well as technical mentorship from AWS solutions architects.

This partnership ensures that even early-stage teams have access to enterprise-grade computing resources and expert guidance. Teams can train custom models on SageMaker, access leading foundation models through Bedrock, and deploy serverless inference pipelines through Lambda — all without worrying about infrastructure costs during the hackathon window. AWS's involvement signals the growing institutional recognition that AI-blockchain convergence is not a niche experiment — it is a fundamental shift in how intelligent systems will be deployed in the future.

Community & Mission

DuckChain's mission extends beyond technology — it is about building a global community of AI-blockchain innovators. Through open-source contributions, developer education programs, and ecosystem grants, DuckChain fosters an environment where the brightest minds can collaborate and create.

The AI Unchained hackathon is the flagship event in DuckChain's community building strategy, bringing together thousands of developers from over 100 countries. Every submission, every team, and every line of code contributes to a growing body of open knowledge that accelerates the entire field of decentralized AI. Beyond the hackathon itself, DuckChain supports winners with follow-on grants, ecosystem introductions, and dedicated engineering mentorship to help the best ideas reach their full potential.

DuckChain's open-source repositories, public developer forums, and regular community calls ensure that no builder is left to figure things out alone. The community is the product, and DuckChain is committed to nurturing it for the long term.
