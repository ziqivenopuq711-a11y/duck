DuckChain × AWS Launch $1.1M Hackathon: AI Unchained

A worldwide AI × Web3 hackathon uniting engineers, creators, and pioneers — August 25 through September 1, 2025

The Hackathon Launch

SINGAPORE, Aug. 29, 2025 — DuckChain has partnered with Amazon Web Services (AWS) to officially launch Hack: AI Unchained — a worldwide, week-long hackathon that unites AI engineers, Web3 creators, and decentralized infrastructure pioneers. Scheduled to take place from August 25 through September 1, 2025, the event stands as one of the most significant AI × Web3 convergence events of the year.

Featuring a combined prize pool surpassing $1.1 million (comprising $1,000,000 in DUCK liquidity backing, $100,000 in DUCK token rewards, and $10,000 in AWS cloud credits), the hackathon establishes a competitive environment where teams can prototype, innovate, and deploy cutting-edge AI-native solutions.

The Premier Arena for AI × Web3 Innovation

Participants can compete across four primary challenge categories:

🤖

AI Agents & Autonomous Apps

Creating self-directed applications driven by intelligent AI logic that operate autonomously on-chain without human intermediaries.

🌐

Decentralized AI Infrastructure

Investigating DePIN architectures, federated learning techniques, and distributed storage systems for resilient AI deployment.

⛓️

On-chain AI-as-a-Service

Bringing large language models, generative AI systems, and APIs natively on-chain for trustless, permissionless AI execution.

🛠️

DuckChain Native Tools & Agent SDK

Harnessing DuckChain's comprehensive developer toolkit to power next-gen agentic solutions with full blockchain integration.

In addition to the prize money, all participants will receive access to AI development tools and resources from ecosystem partners including ChainGPT and DuckChain, such as APIs, SDKs, and practical case studies designed to accelerate development.
