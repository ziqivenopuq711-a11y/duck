Prize Details

Over $1.1 million in DUCK rewards, liquidity support, and AWS cloud credits up for grabs

Total Prize Pool

The AI Unchained hackathon features one of the most substantial prize pools in the history of AI-blockchain hackathons, with total rewards exceeding $1.1 million distributed across multiple categories. The prize structure is designed to reward excellence across different types of innovation, from cutting-edge AI agent technology to elegant developer tooling.

Prizes are distributed in DUCK tokens and AWS cloud credits, giving winners tangible resources to continue building after the hackathon concludes. Beyond monetary rewards, winning teams gain visibility with DuckChain's ecosystem partners, investor network, and global community of over 150,000 developer-focused followers. The combination of financial rewards, ecosystem introductions, and media exposure makes AI Unchained one of the most valuable hackathons to win in the entire blockchain space.

Category Breakdown

The prize pool is distributed across four major competitive tracks. The AI Agent Track offers $400,000 in DUCK rewards for teams building the most sophisticated autonomous agents on DuckChain. The DeFAI Track awards $300,000 to projects that most innovatively combine AI with decentralized finance protocols.

The Infrastructure & Tooling Track provides $200,000 for developer tools, oracles, bridges, and other foundational technology. The Creative & Wild Card Track distributes $100,000 to the most unexpected and inventive AI-blockchain use cases that defy categorization. Additionally, $100,000 in DUCK liquidity support is available to all qualifying projects, and $10,000 in AWS cloud credits supports teams that build on AWS infrastructure.

AWS Credits & Resources

All teams that submit a qualifying project to the AI Unchained hackathon are eligible for AWS cloud computing credits. Finalist teams receive $10,000 in AWS credits that can be used across the full suite of AWS services including EC2 for compute, S3 for storage, SageMaker for machine learning, and Bedrock for foundation model access.

These credits do not expire at the end of the hackathon, giving teams sustained runway to continue developing and scaling their projects. AWS technical mentors are available throughout the hackathon to help teams architect their cloud infrastructure efficiently and take advantage of the most relevant AWS services for their use case. For teams that go on to build production applications after the hackathon, AWS's Activate program provides additional resources and support for blockchain startups.

Additional Benefits

Beyond monetary prizes, AI Unchained winners and top finalists receive a rich package of additional benefits. DuckChain offers ecosystem grants for the most promising projects, with grants ranging from $50,000 to $500,000 in DUCK tokens available to teams that commit to building on DuckChain after the hackathon.

Top projects are also introduced to DuckChain's VC partner network including Oak Grove Ventures, GeekCartel, and Inception Capital, providing access to potential seed funding. Winners receive featured coverage on DuckChain's media channels, invitations to present at DuckChain's annual developer conference, and mentorship from DuckChain's core engineering team to help bring their projects to production. The goal is not just to reward the best hackathon builds — it is to provide the foundation for those builds to become lasting, impactful products in the DuckChain ecosystem.
